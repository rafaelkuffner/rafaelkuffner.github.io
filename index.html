About me

Hi! I'm Rafael. I'm a PhD student at Técnico Lisboa   (where i also took my BsC and MsC in Information Systems and Computer Science). There, I work with the Visualization and Intelligent Multimodal Interfaces Group at INESC-ID Lisbon. However, my main research position is working in the Blackbox Project at FCSH , where my PhD research (and more) is being applied to dance and performing arts scenarios. Current interest topics are video-based rendering, point cloud processing/rendering and recently also VR and AR.

For more details about who I am, what I like, and what I do,  I'd encourage clicking the social networks referenced near my portrait.
